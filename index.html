1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information Smart Life Assistant collects, how it is collected, how it is used, stored and protected.

2. Information We Collect

  • Profile information voluntarily entered by users.
  • Health and wellness information voluntarily entered by users, including height, weight, body measurements, water intake, sleep, exercise and goals.
  • Device information, diagnostics and crash logs.
  • Advertising identifiers used by advertising providers.

3. How We Collect and Use Information

  • Information is collected only when users enter it into the application or when required for app analytics and stability.
  • Used to calculate health metrics, generate charts, provide reminders and improve app performance.
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple.

4. AI Features

Smart Life Assistant includes AI-powered features.

  • To generate AI responses, your chat messages will be sent to Google Gemini, a third-party AI service.

5. Advertising

The app may display ads through Google AdMob. Advertising identifiers may be used to measure advertising performance.

6. Analytics

Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics may collect anonymous usage statistics, crash reports and device performance information. No personally identifiable health information is shared for analytics.

7. Data Storage

User data is primarily stored on the user's device. Data is protected using appropriate security measures.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crash Reporting)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Apple App Store Services (In‑App Purchases & Subscription Management)

9. Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed securely through Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment card information.

10. Health Disclaimer
